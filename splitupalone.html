Alone in the Hallway

You decide to stay on your own.

You sprint down the hallway in the opposite direction. Heart thumping and the sounds of screams and groans echoing off the walls.

Suddenly, something blocks your path. You twist and turn, changing direction as fast as you can. You feel the tension of the steps behind you inching closer.

At the end of the hallway, the library comes into view. You dash toward it, hoping it will offer some safety.
