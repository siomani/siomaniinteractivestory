Surrounded

You catch up to the group and throw yourself into the fight.

For a moment, it feels like you might push them back.

Then you tear free.

The zombies turn all at once. Mouths open. Arms reach. They start chasing.

You run with the group toward the stairwell. Feet pound. The metal steps creak beneath the weight of everyone fleeing upward.

Zombies pour in from different directions, blocking hallways, stumbling down stairs, forcing you to fight just to keep moving.

Someone shouts over the noise, maybe you should find a classroom. Somewhere to regroup. Somewhere to breathe. Somewhere to make a plan.
