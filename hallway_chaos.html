Hallway Chaos

The hallway is packed.

Students push past you. Someone falls. Someone doesn’t get back up.

At first, it looks like a fight. Students shoving, clawing, screaming at each other.

Then you look closer.

Skin torn. Eyes empty. Blood. Mouths snapping where words should be.

The hallway isn’t full of students fighting, it’s full of zombies.

You see a small group of students struggling. They’re barely holding them back.

You freeze. Should you help them? Or could it get you killed...

Staying here feels dangerous. Moving feels dangerous too.

Help the group Run away
